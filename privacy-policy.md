developed by Saadet HARUN.

users.

The App does not request or collect any personally identifiable
information. No user data is transmitted to external servers.

provided by the operating system. This data does not include personal
information.

## Service Providers

The App does not use third-party services that collect user data.

## Security

Since the App does not collect any personal information, there is no
risk of personal data leakage from the App.

## Children's Information

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from
children under the age of 13.
